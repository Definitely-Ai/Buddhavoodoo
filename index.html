About Buddha Voodoo

Buddha Voodoo is a yoga space with its own heartbeat—welcoming, playful, a little wild, and absolutely real. It’s not about perfection, pretzel poses, or trying to fit into some idea of what yoga “should” look like.

It’s about showing up exactly as you are and discovering what your body can do when it’s supported instead of judged.

No Ego. Just Yoga.

Who She Is

Jodi Ziajka

The energy behind Buddha Voodoo. She’s one of those people who instantly makes you feel comfortable—funny, grounded, compassionate, and honest.

Jodi teaches from a place of lived experience, not some textbook version of wellness. She loves movement, she loves people, and she believes yoga should feel like coming home to yourself… not like trying to impress the room.

She’s the kind of teacher who laughs with you, challenges you when you’re ready, and supports you when you’re not. Nothing about her is intimidating—just warm, real, and genuinely invested in helping every body feel good in their own skin.

Jodi

What She Does

Buddha Voodoo blends traditional yoga foundations with a relaxed, down-to-earth teaching style that invites everyone in—no matter your age, size, flexibility, or experience level.

Natural Movement

Moving in ways that feel natural to your body.

Gentle Strength

Building strength gently and sustainably.

Stress Release

Letting go of what you don't need to carry.

Self-Connection

Creating space for breath, rest, and listening.

There’s no “right” way here—just your way.

The Feel

Imagine stepping into a room where the energy is welcoming, the vibe is a little funky and a lot of fun, and you can relax, breathe, move, and just be yourself.

That’s Buddha Voodoo.

It’s yoga without ego. Movement without pressure. Community without judgment. A place where spirituality meets playfulness, where the classes feel as unique as the logo—balanced, bold, and a little rebellious.

It’s yoga for every body… truly.
